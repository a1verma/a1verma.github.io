Ashwin Verma

I am a Postdoctoral Research Assistant in the Department of Electrical & Computer Enginerring at the Purdue University, working with Prof. Vijay Gupta. I am interested in theoretical aspects of distributed optimization, learning and applied probability theory. Recently, I have been working on reinforcement learning with communication constraints and causal inference in games.

I obtained my Ph.D. from the University of California San Diego in 2024, where I had the fortune of working with Prof. Behrouz Touri. Previously, I obtained my B.Tech and M.Tech (with Prof. R. K. Bansal) in Electrical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur(IIT Kanpur).
